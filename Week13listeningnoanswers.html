PART 2 – WRITING

Essay Task

You must answer this question. Write your answer in 220–260 words in an appropriate style.

Your class has had a discussion about the advantages and disadvantages of different places to live. You have made the notes below:

  • Living in the city versus living in the country
  • employment opportunities
  • lifestyle and leisure
  • environment

Some opinions expressed in the discussion:

“There are always more jobs and better career prospects in the city.”
“Life in the country is healthier and less stressful.”
“Cities offer more cultural activities and chances to meet new people.”

Write an essay discussing two of the factors in your notes. You should explain which place — the city or the country — you think is better overall for young people, giving reasons in support of your answer.
